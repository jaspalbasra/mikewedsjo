A Night of Music & Joy

The Sangeet is one of the most vibrant and joyous celebrations in Indian weddings. It's an evening filled with music, dancing, singing, and pure celebration as both families come together to honor the upcoming union.

Come prepared for loud music, singing and dancing! We'll have a Detroit Style Party with our DJ spinning Punjabi, Indian and American tunes that will keep everyone on the dance floor.

This is your chance to let loose, celebrate love, and create unforgettable memories with family and friends!
